Malarious


Recently, Peace Corps/Senegal's partner Malaria No More invited 5 comedians—Ed Helms, Paul Sheer, Nick Kroll, Natasha Leggero, and Riki Lindhomme—to Senegal to learn more about malaria and to produce a series of web-based films to raise awareness about the importance of malaria prevention, in a, well, unorthodox way. They visited Peace Corps Volunteers and participated in a Volunteer-organized bed net distribution and education campaign in the village of Dindefelo. The result of their work, coming in October, is: Malarious: Hollywood Bites Back! Check back soon for the films.
