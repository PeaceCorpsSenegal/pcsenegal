Technical Training

Welcome to the Technical training page! On this page, you will find useful documents that will help you prepare for the technical portion of your training as a Peace Corps Volunteer in Senegal. All of the material contained in these documents may be included in your pre arrival techincal exam. Please, make use of the resources below, and send us your feedback or questions.

Tech Documents

Prospere Project Plan

The Prospere Project Plan contains all of the goals, indicators, and methods you will use to complete your work. This document is divided into specific areas of work, and gives clear numerical guidelines to monitor the countryâ€™s progress.

Ecology of Senegal

This document will introduce you to the different ecological zones of Senegal, and their effect on local populations. Using the maps and information in this document you can familiarize yourselves with the populations, lifestyles, and challenges faced by people across Senegal.

Ministry of Health Overview

Working as a Health/Environment Volunteer, you will need to be familiar with the administrative structure and personnel of the Senegalese Ministry of Health. This file contains organizational charts, administrative breakdowns, translations of positions, and external reports on all the aspects of the Ministry. The information in this document will ensure that you are able to have informed interactions with your local health officials.

Senegalese Education System

The Senegalese education system is based off of the French model, with which very few Americans are familiar. This article will give you a breakdown of the educational administrative structure, the age/grade equivalents to the US education system, and a breakdown of pedagogical methods and challenges faced by Senegalese teachers.
