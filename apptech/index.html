Appropriate Technology

PC Senegal loves testing appropriate technologies. We welcome partnerships with all organizations that give us a chance to use our knowledge of local communities, languages, and lifestyles to help with design and testing. If you have a technology, we have the on the ground experience to put it through its paces. Contact us if you have one you'd like us to experiment with. Below you'll find some technologies we've already been extending.

52 Pumps in 52 Weeks
Join PCVs Marcie Todd and Garrison Harward as they take on the challenge of installing fifty-two appropriate technologies rope pumps at fifty-two sites over the course of fifty-two weeks.
