The Fully Belly Project intends to improve life for rural communities with education and technology. This project trains people to use technologies such as the Universal Nut Sheller to increase incomes and make rural work more efficient. It is a non-profit organization working to improve the economic situation for people all over the globe, and creating new technologies that rural people can benefit from.
