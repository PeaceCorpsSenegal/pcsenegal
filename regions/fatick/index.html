Fatick

Part of Peace Corps Kaolack, the region of Fatick runs along the coast south of Dakar, all the way south to the Gambian border. Volunteers in the region are predominantly Sereer and Wolof speakers, with a few Mandinka volunteers thrown in. All sectors are represented in the region, and the number of volunteers in the area is increasing with each incoming stage.

For more information, please see the regional page for Kaolack.
