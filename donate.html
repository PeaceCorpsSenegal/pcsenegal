Donate

You can support the work of Peace Corps Volunteers in Senegal by donating through the Peace Corps website.

To support all Peace Corps programs in Senegal, you can donate to the Peace Corps Senegal Country Fund. Donating to the Country Fund allows Peace Corps Senegal to support the highest priority projects.

Or you can look at current Volunteer projects seeking funding through the Peace Corps Partnership Program. Funds received through Partnership funding go toward the project to which you donate.
