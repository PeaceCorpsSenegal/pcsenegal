Segou Ecolodge Project

Welcome Summary History Budget and Funding Sustainability About Segou Ecotourism

The Ségou Ecolodge Project is a collaborative effort between the Ségou Campement Groupement and the Peace Corps. It is a locally driven ecotourism venture that will be a flagship example of environmental, financial, and social sustainability. Local materials and local labor were used in construction, 15% of the profits are given to the community, it provides an important source of employment for the village, and 35% of the profits are reserved for repairing and expanding.
