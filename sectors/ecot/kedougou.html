Kedougou

Niokolo | Salemata | Bandafassi | Dindefelo

Kedougou region, in the far south-east of Senegal on the Guinean and Malian frontiers is the premier adventure vacation destination in Senegal. With its many waterfalls including the largest in Senegal at Dindefelo, the mountains along the Guinean plateau, and its many bush-trails, the Kedougou region is perfect for those looking for an escape into the natural beauty of Africa. Bordering the National Park of Niokola Koba, Kedougou is a perfect base for safaris to see the various species of birds, monkeys, hippos, crocodiles, and the rare Senegalese Lion.
