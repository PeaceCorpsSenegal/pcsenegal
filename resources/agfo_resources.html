Agrofrestry Resources

Agroforestry Workshop Course Catalog
This is a list of workshop lessons that are available pertaining to agroforestry. These sessions are open to anyone who would like to learn. Like the language workshops, we would ask that there be at least three people interested in the lesson, and pick at least three of the lessons as to make at least a full day.


Live Fencing Pocket Manual

The live fencing manual is an in-depth explanation of the principles and application of live fencing. This manual includes photos, diagrams, sample data, a case study, and a listing of preferred trees for use in the field. Designed for use by non-Agfos, this manual is a must for anyone working with live fencing.


Tree Identification Manual

This is a full color photo tree identification manual for tree species we can find here in Senegal and west africa. Original photos came from the Trees and Shrubs of the African Sahel manual and have been compiled into just pictures with scientific names.


Agroforestry Manual

PC/Senegal Agroforestry volunteers revised and updated the PC/The Gambia Agroforestry Manual to contain new information and photos pertaining specifically to Senegal.


Moringa Pocket Guide

A short booklet created by Peace Corps Senegal that describes basic propagation methods and uses for Moringa oleifera, one of the trees most commonly planted by volunteers in Senegal.


Naka Lanuy Jëmbëte Garab?

An illustrated manual in Wolof about basic tree propagation, created by Peace Corps Senegal Agroforestry Volunteers in 2009.


Documents from the 2009 Peace Corps Africa Food Security Conference
