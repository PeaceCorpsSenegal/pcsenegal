West Africa Peace Corps Volunteer Conference

Sharing Best Practices from the Field

December 3-4, 2010

Thies, Senegal

Overview

The West Africa Peace Corps Volunteer Conference: Sharing Best Practices from the Field, hosted by Peace Corps/Senegal, was an opportunity for Peace Corps Volunteers from around West Africa to share innovative ideas and lessons learned in grassroots development. Volunteers from Burkina Faso, Cape Verde, the Gambia, Mali, Senegal, and Togo gathered at the Peace Corps Training Center in Senegal to present best practices, conduct workshops, discuss new ideas in roundtable discussions, and view appropriate technology presentations.
