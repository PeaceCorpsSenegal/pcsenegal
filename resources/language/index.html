Language Resources

Senegal is home to an abundance of local languages, developed over the thousands of years before colonization. Peace Corps Volunteers study and speak many of them as part of their service, and most Volunteers conduct a majority of their business using their local language. Volunteers utilize the following languages during their services:

  • French
  • Wolof
  • Pulaar
  • Sereer
  • Mandinka
  • Jaxanke
  • Bassari
For more information on any one particular language, use the menu to your left.
