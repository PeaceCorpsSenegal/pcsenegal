SPA - Small Projects Assistance

SPA Grants can be used to fund projects, training workshops, and technical exchanges in the following Program Elements: 1) Malaria Prevention, 2) Basic Education, 3) Economic Growth, 4) Agricultural Productivity, 5) Natural Resources and Biodiversity

The date of the next SPA Grant Review Meeting can be found on the Peace Corps Calendar.

Food Security

Food Security grants can be used to 1) strengthen the skills of farmers, 2) construct school gardens, 3) promote nutrition education, and 4) enable communities to gain more of a benefit from the food that is already grown in Senegal.

Food Security grant proposals are reviewed at the SPA Grant Review Meeting.

The date of the next SPA Grant Review Meeting can be found on the Peace Corps Calendar.
